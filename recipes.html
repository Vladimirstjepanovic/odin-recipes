Pizza Margherita or Margherita pizza is a typical
Neapolitan pizza, roundish in shape with a raised edge and garnished with hand-crushed
peeled tomatoes, mozzarella, fresh basil leaves, and extra
virgin olive oil. The dough is made by mixing water,
salt and yeast with flour.
